Our Reputation is Built on Decades of Service

We have been in existence for more than 50 years and produce oil and gas, primarily in the Appalachian Basin. We enjoy an outstanding reputation due to our commitment to excellence, and integrity in our dealings with our landowner partners and industry partners.

Environmental Stewardship

Landowner Commitment

We have a sincere respect for the environment and make every effort to minimize the impact of our operations to landowners. We treat the land where we lease as if it were our own, and strive to leave it better than we find it. We don't see oil and gas exploration and environmental stewardship as mutually exclusive.
